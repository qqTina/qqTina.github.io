Hi! I'm Qianqian Tao, UCSD 23er. I'm from China.


Words are usually not my expression. Instead, I like to create hand-made mediums to tell who I am. I started to sew dolls and purses when I was a student of grade 2. Ever since then, I always sent accessories that I made to people around me as birthday gifts. All the little decorations embody my spirit at the time. When it comes to graph design, I first found it a taxing challenge to utilize these drawing tools. Through train and trial, I gradually become accustomed to these tools. Even though I'm still an amateur, I would be pervious to all the techniques and designs.
