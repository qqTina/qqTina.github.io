Introduction

The International Genetically Engineered Machine (iGEM) competition is a worldwide synthetic biology competition. Our project is about developing a new and efficient method for degrading endogenous proteins in mammalian cells. We created PR PREDATOR, which is the plasmid that expresses Trim-21 and recombinant antibody. In the team, I was in the Human Practice Group and my main job was art design. Team Wiki

Realizing that phone game is now becoming the most popular way to entertain and relax, I created the game "PR PREDATOR" available in Apple Store in order to spread the significance of Trim in our project. It was hard to design an intricate process into a single game and promote it to people who have no background information. To solve this, I chose the process about Trim finding Nanobody and ubiquitin as the main game.

This is a simplified mechanism for the process. After PR PREDATOR, the modified plasmid, expresses Trim-21 and Nanobody, Trim-21 will combine with the Nanobody. One end of Trim-21 will attract ubiquitin, which facilitates protein degradation. Then, the Nanobody will find the targeted protein and bind with it. The ubiquitin will attracts enzyme to degrade the protein.
